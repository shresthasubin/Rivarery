Negative Impact of Technology in the Watch Industry

While technology has brought significant advancements to the watch industry, it has also introduced several challenges and drawbacks. Below are some of the negative impacts:

Decline in Demand for Traditional Watches

With the rise of smartwatches, the demand for traditional mechanical watches has decreased. Consumers are increasingly opting for smartwatches that offer features like fitness tracking, notifications, and GPS functionality.

Market Saturation

Mass production enabled by technology has led to market saturation, where lower-quality watches flood the market. This affects the perception of premium watch brands and creates tough competition.

Loss of Craftsmanship

As automation takes over, there is a risk of losing traditional watchmaking skills and craftsmanship, which are integral to the heritage and uniqueness of the watch industry.
